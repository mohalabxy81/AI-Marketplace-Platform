# PRD 10 — LEAD MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

> **Status**: Approved
> **Target Audience**: Engineering, Product, Sales Teams
> **Domain**: Marketplace (CRM Sub-Domain)

## 1. Executive Summary
The Lead Management System bridges the gap between discovery and transaction. It captures buyer inquiries from listings and routes them to the appropriate tenant team members. By providing structured tracking of lead statuses (from "New" to "Closed"), this system enables tenants to measure conversion rates, track sales rep performance, and ensure no buyer inquiry falls through the cracks.

## 2. Business Objectives
- **Drive GMV/Conversion**: Maximize the percentage of inquiries that result in successful off-platform or on-platform transactions.
- **Tenant Value**: Prove the platform's ROI to sellers by providing transparent visibility into the leads generated by their subscription or ad spend.
- **Data Capture**: Capture conversion signals to train the discovery ranking models.

## 3. Strategic Goals
- Ensure 100% reliable capture of all lead submissions with zero data loss.
- Provide real-time notification delivery (under 2 seconds) to agents when a lead is captured.
- Establish baseline conversion metrics to train the V2 AI Lead Scoring model.

## 4. User Personas
- **Buyer**: Submits their contact info and message to express interest in a listing.
- **Agent/Sales Rep**: Receives the lead, contacts the buyer, and updates the deal status.
- **Company Admin**: Monitors lead volume, assigns leads, and tracks team response times.

## 5. Stakeholders
- **Analytics Team**: Uses lead conversion data as the primary success metric for the Discovery pipeline.
- **Monetization Team**: Uses lead volume to justify subscription upgrades and CPC ad pricing.

## 6. User Stories
- As a **Buyer**, I want to click one button to contact a seller without having to fill out a massive form.
- As an **Agent**, I want an instant push notification on my phone when a new lead arrives so I can respond while the buyer is still hot.
- As a **Company Admin**, I want to see how many leads each of my sales reps closed this month.
- As a **Sales Rep**, I want to change a lead's status to "Qualified" so my pipeline is organized.

## 7. Functional Requirements
- **FR-LMS-01 (Lead Capture)**: Secure, rate-limited endpoint for capturing buyer inquiries attached to specific listings.
- **FR-LMS-02 (Lead Routing)**: Assign leads to the listing owner by default, with support for manual reassignment.
- **FR-LMS-03 (Status Tracking)**: Maintain a state machine for leads (New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted, Closed-Lost).
- **FR-LMS-04 (Buyer Context)**: Provide the seller with the buyer's public profile and basic context (e.g., search term used, if available).
- **FR-LMS-05 (Spam Prevention)**: Integrate with Trust & Safety to block automated bot submissions.

## 8. Non-Functional Requirements
- **Reliability**: Lead submissions must utilize an Outbox pattern or transactional message queue to guarantee delivery even if notification services fail.
- **Security**: Buyer PII (email, phone) must be strictly access-controlled via RLS and only visible to the assigned tenant.

## 9. User Workflows
- **Lead Submission**: Buyer clicks "Contact" → Submits Form → DB saves Lead record → Event published to Kafka → WebSocket server pushes alert to Seller → Email service sends backup alert to Seller.
- **Lead Progression**: Agent opens Lead → Clicks "Mark Contacted" → DB updates status → Status change logged in timeline.

## 10. State Machines
- **Lead Status**: `NEW` → `CONTACTED` → `QUALIFIED` → `CONVERTED` (Success) OR `CLOSED_LOST` (Failure).

## 11. Business Rules
- A buyer can only submit one active inquiry per listing per 24 hours to prevent spam.
- Leads belonging to suspended tenants are captured but locked, unable to be viewed until the tenant is reactivated.

## 12. Permissions
- `inquiries:create` - Submit a lead (Authenticated Buyers).
- `leads:manage` - View and update leads (Seller/Agent/Admin).

## 13. Events Generated
- `marketplace.inquiry_submitted`
- `marketplace.lead_status_updated`
- `marketplace.lead_assigned`

## 14. Events Consumed
- `trust.fraud_detected` (Can block lead submission if buyer is flagged).

## 15. Analytics Requirements
- Track Time-to-First-Response (TTFR) by seller.
- Measure Lead Conversion Rate (Converted / Total Leads) per listing and per tenant.
- Track Lead Source attribution (Did they click from Search, Feed, or Similar Items?).

## 16. KPIs
- Total Leads Generated.
- Platform-Wide Average Conversion Rate.
- Average Seller Response Time.

## 17. Success Metrics
- 99% of leads are successfully delivered and acknowledged by the system.
- 70% of leads receive a status update from the seller within 48 hours.

## 18. Edge Cases
- **Deleted Listing**: If a buyer submits an inquiry exactly as the listing is archived or deleted, gracefully handle the submission and attach it to the archived record so the seller still gets the lead.

## 19. Failure Scenarios
- **WebSocket Failure**: If the real-time push fails, the system must rely on the transactional email fallback to ensure the seller is notified.

## 20. Compliance Requirements
- **GDPR**: If a buyer deletes their account, their PII on historical leads must be anonymized, but the lead record (for conversion analytics) remains.

## 21. Realtime Requirements
- Instantaneous delivery of the `inquiry_submitted` event to the seller's active dashboard via WebSocket.

## 22. AI Requirements
- V2: AI Lead Scoring model (predicting the likelihood to convert based on buyer history and inquiry text) to help reps prioritize their inbox.

## 23. MVP Scope
- Basic inquiry form submission.
- Simple inbox view for sellers.
- Email notification on new lead.

## 24. V1 Scope
- State machine tracking (New → Converted).
- Real-time WebSocket push notifications.
- Buyer profile context.

## 25. V2 Scope
- AI Lead Scoring.
- Automated round-robin lead routing to sales teams.

## 26. Future Enhancements
- Integration with external CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot) via Webhooks.

## 27. Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] A buyer can submit an inquiry on a listing.
- [ ] The seller receives an email and an in-app notification immediately.
- [ ] The seller can view the lead in their dashboard and change its status to "Contacted".
- [ ] Submitting more than 3 identical inquiries in a minute triggers a rate limit/spam block.
